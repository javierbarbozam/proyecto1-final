selina la Fortuna

Situated in Fortuna, 5.9 km from La Fortuna Waterfall, Selina La Fortuna features accommodation with a garden, free private parking, a shared lounge and a restaurant. This 3-star hotel offers a shared kitchen, a tour desk and free WiFi. Guests can make use of a bar.

The daily breakfast offers à la carte, continental or American options.

Guests at the hotel will be able to enjoy activities in and around Fortuna, like cycling.

Kalambu Hot Springs is 5 km from Selina La Fortuna, while Mistico Arenal Hanging Bridges Park is 20 km from the property. The nearest airport is Fortuna Airport, 9 km from the accommodation.

Business travellers particularly like the location — they rated it 9.3 for a work-related trip.
