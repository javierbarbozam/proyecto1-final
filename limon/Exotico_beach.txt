Exôtico Beach & Rooms 

Exôtico Beach & Rooms features an outdoor swimming pool, garden, a terrace and restaurant in Puerto Viejo. With a bar, the 5-star hotel has air-conditioned rooms with free WiFi, each with a private bathroom. Guests can enjoy garden views.

At the hotel, rooms come with a balcony with pool view. At Exôtico Beach & Rooms all rooms come with bed linen and towels.

A buffet breakfast is available every morning at the accommodation.

Manzanillo Beach is 2.7 km from Exôtico Beach & Rooms, while Jaguar Rescue Center is 7.1 km away. The nearest airport is Captain Manuel Niño International Airport, 51 km from the hotel.

Exôtico Beach & Rooms has been welcoming Booking.com guests since 4 Jan 2023.
