Hotel Oceans Two

Located in Manuel Antonio, 1.4 km from Playitas Beach, Hotel Oceans Two provides accommodation with an outdoor swimming pool, free private parking, a garden and a terrace. Each accommodation at the 4-star hotel has sea views, and guests can enjoy access to a restaurant and to a bar. The accommodation features room service, a concierge service and currency exchange for guests. At the hotel every room is fitted with air conditioning, a seating area, a flat-screen TV with cable channels, a safety deposit box and a private bathroom with a shower, free toiletries and a hairdryer. Hotel Oceans Two offers certain units with pool views, and rooms are fitted with a balcony. At the accommodation the rooms include bed linen and towels. A à la carte, continental or American breakfast is available daily at the property. Biesanz is 1.5 km from Hotel Oceans Two, while Espadilla Beach is 1.5 km away. The nearest airport is La Managua, 9 km from the hotel, and the property offers a paid airport shuttle service. Hotel Oceans Two has been welcoming Booking.com guests since 21 Nov 2020.
