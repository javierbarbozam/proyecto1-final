El Faro Beach Hotel

Offering an infinity pool and free WiFi, El Faro Beach Hotel is located in Manuel Antonio, about 100 m walk from the beachfront and 50 from Manuel Antonio National Park. This property is Costa Rica's only container hotel. Each minimalist and modern accommodation will provide you with air conditioning. There is also a coffee machine available. Featuring a shower, private bathroom also comes with a hairdryer. Guests can enjoy the sea and pool views from the rooms. Extras include a safety deposit box and bed linen. At El Faro Beach Hotel you will find a terrace and a bar. Other facilities offered at the property include a tour desk, luggage storage and laundry facilities. The property offers free parking and shuttle services are available for a fee.
