Limón

Stretching along the Caribbean Coast, Limon is considered to be one of the most lush regions of Costa Rica. Boast picturesque white-sand beaches lined with coconut trees for an ultimate tropical paradise. Limon features everything from mangrove, wetlands to towering mountains. Traveling has been creating memorable vacations for thousands of travelers every year. We are a company who cherishes the tropical beauty and unparalleled adventure that Costa Rica has to offer.
