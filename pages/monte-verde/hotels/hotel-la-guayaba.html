Hotel La Guayaba

Located in Monteverde Costa Rica, La Guayaba Monteverde features a garden. The property is around 2.4 km from Sky Adventures Monteverde, 3.7 km from Selvatura Adventure Park and an 11-minute walk from Monteverde Ecological Sanctuary. Free WiFi is available and Monteverde Cloud Forest Biological Reserve is 3.6 km away. All rooms in the bed and breakfast are equipped with a TV with cable channels. Rooms are complete with a private bathroom fitted with a shower and free toiletries, while some rooms at La Guayaba Monteverde also boast a patio. All units include a wardrobe. A continental breakfast is served daily at the property. The nearest airport is Tambor Airport, 68 km from the accommodation.
