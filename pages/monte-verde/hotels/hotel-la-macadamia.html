Hotel La Macadamia

Located 4.8 km from Sky Adventures Monteverde, La Macadamia - Monteverde provides accommodation with a garden, barbecue facilities and room service for your convenience. A fridge, a microwave and toaster are also provided, as well as a coffee machine and a kettle. Selvatura Adventure Park is 6.9 km from the villa, while Monteverde Orchid Garden is 1.7 km away.
