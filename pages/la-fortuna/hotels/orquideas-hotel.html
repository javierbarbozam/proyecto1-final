Orquideas Boutique Hotel

Located in Fortuna, 4.4 km from La Fortuna Waterfall, Orquideas Boutique Hotel provides accommodation with an outdoor swimming pool, free private parking, a garden and a restaurant. The property is situated 6.5 km from Kalambu Hot Springs, 22 km from Mistico Arenal Hanging Bridges Park and 23 km from Sky Adventures Arenal. The accommodation features a hot tub, free WiFi throughout the property and family rooms. Rooms are complete with a private bathroom equipped with a bath or shower, guest rooms at the hotel have a flat-screen TV and air conditioning, and selected rooms are equipped with a balcony. All rooms will provide guests with a fridge. Guests at Orquideas Boutique Hotel can enjoy a à la carte breakfast.Venado Caves is 26 km from the accommodation, while Ecoglide Arenal Park is 5.9 km away. The nearest airport is Fortuna Airport, 9 km from Orquideas Boutique Hotel. Business travellers particularly like the location — they rated it 9.6 for a work-related trip.
