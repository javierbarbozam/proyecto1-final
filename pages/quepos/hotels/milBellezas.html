Hacienda Mil Bellezas

Boasting a swimming pool, a garden, a terrace and views of the garden, Hacienda Mil Bellezas is set in Quepos and offers accommodation with free WiFi. Some of the units feature a satellite TV, a fully equipped kitchen with a fridge, and a private bathroom with a shower and a hairdryer. The daily breakfast offers à la carte, continental or Full English/Irish options. If you would like to discover the area, hiking is possible in the surroundings. Manuel Antonio National Park is 26 km from the holiday home, while Rainmaker Costa Rica is 16 km from the property. The nearest airport is La Managua, 21 km from Hacienda Mil Bellezas, and the property offers a paid airport shuttle service. Hacienda Mil Bellezas has been welcoming Booking.com guests since 28 Feb 2019.
