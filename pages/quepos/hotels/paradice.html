Pacific Paradise Resort

Pacific Paradise Resort is situated in Quepos, 8 km from Manuel Antonio National Park. Guests can enjoy the on-site restaurant. Free private parking is available on site. Rooms are fitted with a TV with satellite channels. All rooms are fitted with a private bathroom. Pacific Paradise Resort features free WiFi throughout the property. Canopy is 500 m from Pacific Paradise Resort. The nearest airport is Tobías Bolaños International Airport, 57 km from the property. Pacific Paradise Resort has been welcoming Booking.com guests since 9 Sept 2015.
