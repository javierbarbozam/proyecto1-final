Quepos

Quepos, a small town in Puntarenas province, is located on Costa Rica’s central Pacific coast. Offers vacationers a wealth of activities to do as well. Has many fine restaurants, art galleries and souvenir shops, most within a short walking distance. Book an exhilarating canopy tour, go horseback riding, kayaking and rafting.
