Adventure Park & Hotel Vista Golfo

Located in the hills near Miramar, this hotel offers amazing views over the forest as far as the Gulf of Nicoya. It offers an outdoor pool and a range of adventure activities. Adventure Park & Hotel Vista Golfo has a low and high ropes course and offers ziplining over waterfalls and the forest canopy. You can also go horse riding and quad biking. The El Tucán Restaurant serves international food and offers fantastic views. There is also a terrace where you can enjoy a drink with the impressive backdrop of the rainforest. The hotel offers a shuttle service from various places including San José and Liberia. You can drive to the coast in around 30 minutes. Adventure Park & Hotel Vista Golfo has been welcoming Booking.com guests since 8 Nov 2011.
