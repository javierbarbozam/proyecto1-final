Hacienda Sassenberg

Situated in Miramar, 34 km from Parque Marino del Pacifico, Hacienda Sassenberg has an outdoor swimming pool, a garden and a terrace, as well as free WiFi. The accommodation provides a tour desk, and currency exchange for guests. At the guest house, the rooms are equipped with a wardrobe, bed linen and a patio with a mountain view. Hacienda Sassenberg features some units that have sea views, and the rooms are fitted with a private bathroom with a shower. All rooms will provide guests with a fridge. The daily breakfast offers buffet, continental or vegetarian options. Guests at the accommodation will be able to enjoy activities in and around Miramar, like hiking. The nearest airport is Juan Santamaría International, 97 km from Hacienda Sassenberg, and the property offers a paid airport shuttle service.
