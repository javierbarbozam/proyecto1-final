House of Nomad

House of Nomad - Adults only in Tamarindo has 4-star accommodation with a garden and a terrace. With an outdoor swimming pool, the 4-star hotel has air-conditioned rooms with free WiFi, each with a private bathroom. The property is allergy-free and is situated 500 m from Tamarindo Beach. At the hotel rooms have bed linen and towels. Grande Beach is 1.5 km from House of Nomad - Adults only, while Langosta Beach is 1.9 km from the property. The nearest airport is Tamarindo, 4 km from the accommodation, and the property offers a paid airport shuttle service. This is our guests' favourite part of Tamarindo, according to independent reviews. House of Nomad - Adults only has been welcoming Booking.com guests since 10 Nov 2022.
