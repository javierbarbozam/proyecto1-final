W Costa Rica Resort

The rooms and suites at the hotel are fitted with a seating area, a flat-screen TV with cable channels and a private bathroom with free toiletries and a shower. All rooms at W Costa Rica include a private balcony and each room comes with a coffee machine. All rooms at the accommodation have air conditioning and a wardrobe. A buffet breakfast is served daily at the property. The area is popular for cycling, and car hire is available at W Costa Rica Resort Hotel. Tamarindo is 20 km from the hotel, while Playa Hermosa is 36 km away. Tamarindo Airport is 16 km from the property W Costa Rica Resort – Playa Conchal has been welcoming Booking.com guests since 17 May 2018. Hotel chain/brand: W Hotels
