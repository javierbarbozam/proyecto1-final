Loma Real Hotel & Hot Springs

Located in Fortuna, 6.7 km from La Fortuna Waterfall, Loma Real Hotel & Hot Springs provides accommodation with an outdoor swimming pool, free private parking and a garden. The property is situated 16 km from Mistico Arenal Hanging Bridges Park, 17 km from Sky Adventures Arenal and 20 km from Venado Caves. Providing free WiFi throughout the property, the non-smoking hotel features a hot spring bath.

At the hotel, each room comes with a desk, a flat-screen TV, a private bathroom, bed linen, towels and a patio with a garden view. Certain rooms include a kitchen with a toaster. Guest rooms will provide guests with a fridge.

A à la carte, continental or vegetarian breakfast is available daily at the property.

Popular points of interest near Loma Real Hotel & Hot Springs include Kalambu Hot Springs, Ecotermales Fortuna and Ecoglide Arenal Park. The nearest airport is Fortuna Airport, 14 km from the accommodation.

Loma Real Hotel & Hot Springs has been welcoming Booking.com guests since 25 Aug 2015.
