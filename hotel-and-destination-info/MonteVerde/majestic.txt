Majestic Lodge Luxury

Located in El Castillo de la Fortuna, Majestic Lodge Luxury and Spacious Home with Private Pool and Jacuzzi is a holiday home boasting a year-round outdoor pool. The air-conditioned unit is 13 km from Fortuna.

There is a dining area and a kitchen equipped with oven. A flat-screen TV is provided. Other facilities at Majestic Lodge Luxury and Spacious Home with Private Pool and Jacuzzi include a hot tub and barbecue.

Car hire is available at the property and the area is popular for horse riding. Fortuna is 24 km from Casa Mirador, while Monteverde is 53 km from the property. Juan Santamaría International Airport is 139 km away.

Majestic Lodge Luxury and Spacious Home with Private Pool and Jacuzzi has been welcoming Booking.com guests since 28 Sept 2017.
