Hotel Heliconia

Hotel Heliconia is located in Monteverde Costa Rica, and features a hot tub, an extensive garden, 2 restaurants, a spa centre, massages services and free Wi-Fi.

The suites and rooms present rustic décor with wooden furnishing, a desk, fan, wardrobe and garden views. The bathrooms are private and come with shower. Some of the accommodations include bath.

The on-site restaurants serves Italian cuisine, Mediterranean dishes and local specialities. There is also a bar with international drinks.

Hotel Heliconia offers a laundry room and ironing service, currency exchange and a tour desk which can arrange canopy tours, mountain biking, bird watching and horse riding. Visits to Monteverde Cheese Factory, coffee plantations and Selvatura Park can also be arranged.

This chalet-style hotel is just 3 hours’ drive from San José city centre and 2.5 hours from Juan Santamaría International Airport.

This is our guests' favourite part of Monteverde Costa Rica, according to independent reviews.

Hotel Heliconia - Monteverde has been welcoming Booking.com guests since 21 Feb 2013.
