La Macadamia

Located 4.8 km from Sky Adventures Monteverde, La Macadamia - Monteverde provides accommodation with a garden, barbecue facilities and room service for your convenience. Free WiFi is available throughout the property.

Each unit is equipped with a terrace offering mountain views, a cable flat-screen TV, a well-fitted kitchen and a private bathroom with shower, free toiletries and a hairdryer. A fridge, a microwave and toaster are also provided, as well as a coffee machine and a kettle.

Selvatura Adventure Park is 6.9 km from the villa, while Monteverde Orchid Garden is 1.7 km away. The nearest airport is Fortuna Airport, 84 km from La Macadamia - Monteverde.

La Macadamia - Monteverde has been welcoming Booking.com guests since 11 Aug 2020.
