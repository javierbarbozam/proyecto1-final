new_caribe:

Offering an outdoor pool and water sports facilities, New Caribe Point is located in Cahuita in the Limon Region. Puerto Viejo is 14 km away. Free WiFi is offered throughout the property.

The pet-friendly accommodation is air conditioned and features a seating area. All units include a terrace and/or balcony with pool and garden views. There is also a kitchen, equipped with an oven and microwave. A refrigerator and coffee machine are also provided. Each unit features a private bathroom with a bath or shower. Towels and bed linen are featured.

New Caribe Point features a hot tub and offer massage services.

Bike hire is available at the property and the area is popular for horse riding and snorkeling. A selection of activities are offered in the area, such as windsurfing, diving and cycling. Cocles is 17 km from New Caribe Point. Free private parking is available on site.

This is our guests' favourite part of Cahuita, according to independent reviews.

New Caribe Point has been welcoming Booking.com guests since 18 Jan 2016.
