Les Voiles Blanches

Set in Tamarindo, Les Voiles Blanches - Luxury Lodge offers an outdoor swimming pool, garden and terrace. Free WiFi is available throughout the property and on-site free parking is provided.

At the bed and breakfast, every room comes with a desk and a flat-screen TV with Netflix. Each room includes a private bathroom, all rooms are fitted with a terrace. The units at Les Voiles Blanches - Luxury Lodge have air conditioning and a wardrobe.

A continental breakfast is served each morning at the property. An array of activities can be enjoyed in the surroundings, horse riding, sailing cruise and diving.

Playa Conchal is 13 km from the accommodation, while Playa Avellanas is 10 km away. The nearest airport is Tamarindo Airport, 5 km from the property.

Business travellers particularly like the location — they rated it 9.6 for a work-related trip.

Les Voiles Blanches - Luxury Lodge has been welcoming Booking.com guests since 9 Aug 2015.
