The Oaks Tamarindo

The Oaks Tamarindo Pool Front Condominiums fast wifi is located 10 km from Tamarindo Beach and 5 minutes’ drive from Huacas Village. It features modern design, an extensive garden, sun terrace with swimming pool and free Wi-Fi.

The modern-style apartments are equipped with air conditioning, a fan, a living room with sofa bed, one to three flat screen TVs and a balcony or patio with garden and pool views. The kitchens include coffee maker, microwave, fridge, stove and dining area; while the bathrooms come with shower and free toiletries. Daily maid service is not included.

Guests at this luxury condominium resort will find a variety of food places within 5 minutes drive. Daily maid service is not included. There are also BBQ facilities at two of the three pools.

This pet-friendly complex can arrange tours to Rincón de la Vieja Volcano, Palo Verde National Park, Flamingo Beach and Catamaran trips.

The Oaks Tamarindo Pool Front Condominiums fast wifi is 50 minutes’ drive from Daniel Oduber International Airport and a 1-hour drive from Liberia Town Centre.

Business travellers particularly like the location — they rated it 8.6 for a work-related trip.

The Oaks Tamarindo Pool Front Condominiums fast wifi has been welcoming Booking.com guests since 1 Mar 2013.
