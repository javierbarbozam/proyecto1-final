Los Altos de Eros

Offering an large swimming pool overlooking the Pacific Ocean, plus a spa and wellness centre, Los Altos de Eros is located 10 km from Playa Avellana beach, set on a hill side 20 minutes' drive outside Tamarindo. Free WiFi access is available in public areas.

The rooms here will provide you with air conditioning and a terrace. Private bathrooms also come with towels. All the rooms offer attractive views of either the pool area, the surrounding forest, or the oceanfront.

Cuisine is a major feature of this boutique resort.

At Los Altos de Eros you will find a fitness centre. Other facilities offered include a shared lounge and a tour desk.

An array of activities can be enjoyed on site or in the surroundings, including hiking. The spa offers 5 open-air treatment rooms, a Balinese bath and a large, heated hydrotherapy hot tub. Shuttle services to the airport and towards the local beaches will be provided.

Los Altos de Eros is 11 km from Playa Mansita beach and 15 km from Playa Blanca beach. Daniel Oduber International Airport is 70 km away.

Los Altos de Eros - Adults Only has been welcoming Booking.com guests since 14 Mar 2014.
