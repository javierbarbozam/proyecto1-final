Palm Coast Luxury Rentals

Set in Esterillos Este, Palm Coast Luxury Rentals offers accommodation with a terrace or a balcony, free WiFi and flat-screen TV, as well as an outdoor swimming pool and a garden.

All units comprise a seating area with a sofa, a dining area, and a fully equipped kitchen with various cooking facilities, including a fridge, an oven, a dishwasher and a microwave. A stovetop and toaster are also featured, as well as a coffee machine and a kettle.

At the villa you will find a restaurant serving Belgian, French and Italian cuisine. Vegetarian and vegan options can also be requested.

Palm Coast Luxury Rentals offers a barbecue.

A car rental service is available at the accommodation, while hiking can be enjoyed nearby.

Bejuco Beach is 1 km from Palm Coast Luxury Rentals, while Esterillos Beach is 1 km away. The nearest airport is La Managua, 39 km from the villa, and the property offers a paid airport shuttle service.

Palm Coast Luxury Rentals has been welcoming Booking.com guests since 20 Oct 2017.
