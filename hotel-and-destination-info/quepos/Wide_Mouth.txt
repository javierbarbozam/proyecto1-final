Wide Mouth Frog

Set in Quepos, 2.8 km from La Macha Beach, Wide Mouth Frog offers accommodation with an outdoor swimming pool, private parking, a garden and a shared lounge. Among the facilities of this property are a restaurant, a shared kitchen and an ATM, along with free WiFi. Certain units at the property feature a terrace with pool view.

Some rooms come with a kitchen with a fridge, an oven and a microwave. The units have bed linen.

Manuel Antonio National Park is 7.1 km from the hostel, while Marina Pez Vela is less than 1 km away. The nearest airport is La Managua Airport, 5 km from Wide Mouth Frog.

Business travellers particularly like the location — they rated it 9.2 for a work-related trip.

Wide Mouth Frog has been welcoming Booking.com guests since 18 Nov 2016.
