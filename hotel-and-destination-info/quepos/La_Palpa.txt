Hotel La Palapa Eco Lodge Resort

Located in Portalón, 33 km from Manuel Antonio National Park, Hotel La Palapa Eco Lodge Resort provides accommodation with an outdoor swimming pool, free private parking, a garden and a bar. Built in 2007, this 4-star hotel is within 27 km of Marina Pez Vela and 45 km of Rainmaker Costa Rica. The hotel also offers free WiFi as well as a paid airport shuttle service.

At the hotel, each room is equipped with a wardrobe, bed linen and a patio with a garden view. Each room comes with a private bathroom equipped with a shower and free toiletries, while certain rooms include a kitchen fitted with a fridge.

The daily breakfast offers à la carte, American or vegetarian options.

Hotel La Palapa Eco Lodge Resort offers a sun terrace. The area is popular for hiking, and car hire is available at this 4-star hotel.

Alturas Wildlife Sanctuary is 26 km from the accommodation, while Nauyaca Waterfalls is 27 km away. The nearest airport is La Managua Airport, 24 km from Hotel La Palapa Eco Lodge Resort.

Business travellers particularly like the location — they rated it 8.3 for a work-related trip.

Hotel La Palapa Eco Lodge Resort has been welcoming Booking.com guests since 9 Apr 2012.
