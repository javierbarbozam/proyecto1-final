Sueño Tranquilo

Sueño Tranquilo offers pet-friendly accommodation in Quepos. Free private parking is available on site.

All rooms include a flat-screen TV. A terrace or balcony are featured in certain rooms. Each room comes with a private bathroom fitted with a shower. Sueño Tranquilo features free WiFi throughout the property.

There is a 24-hour front desk at the property.

Canopy Safari is 400 m from Sueño Tranquilo, while MidWorld Costa Rica is 11 km away. The nearest airport is Tobías Bolaños International Airport, and 7 km away from La Managua Local Airport.

Business travellers particularly like the location — they rated it 9.0 for a work-related trip.

Sueño Tranquilo has been welcoming Booking.com guests since 1 Feb 2016.
