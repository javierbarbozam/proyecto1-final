Tabulia Tree

Surrounded by native vegetation and local fauna, Tabulia Tree offers comfortable accommodation in Manuel Antonio. The property features an outdoor swimming pool. Free WiFi access is available in all areas.

Rooms at Tabulia Tree afford a rustic style with detailed decorations in dark tones. They are equipped with air conditioning, cable TVs and private bathrooms. All of them offer views of the mountains, and some include private terraces.

There is an onsite restaurant where a daily breakfast is served, and other meals can be requested throughout the day. Guests can also enjoy a wide array of activities in the surroundings, including fishing, snorkelling and canoeing.

The property offers free parking. Juan Santamaría International Airport is 165 km away.

Tabulia Tree has been welcoming Booking.com guests since 27 Nov 2014.
